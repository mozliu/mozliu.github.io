Emily Lu is should be living her dream, attending the prestigious Idaho Writers’ Workshop and living with her girlfriend after escaping their homophobic families. Instead, she wakes from a nightmare every morning, stalked by the monolithic Goddess of Drought. A comic about love, abuse, terrible white writers, and what it means to “go crazy.” Dark slice of life, published June 2017. Developed through <a href="https://www.undiscoveredcountries.com/development-lab">the inaugural Undiscovered Countries Blast Off program in 2017</a>. <a href="https://mozliu.bigcartel.com/product/the-goddess-of-drought">Available as a 48pg zine.</a>

### Content note: Intimate partner violence
